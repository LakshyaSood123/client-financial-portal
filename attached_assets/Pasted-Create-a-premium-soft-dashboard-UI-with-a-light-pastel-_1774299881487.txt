Create a premium soft-dashboard UI with a light pastel blue-gray theme and deep black contrast cards.

Layout:
- A large rounded master container filling most of the canvas
- A tall black vertical sidebar on the left with rounded corners and white outline icons
- A clean top header with a large greeting on the left and small utility icons plus avatar on the right
- Main content arranged in a modular dashboard grid
- Top row with 4 small KPI cards and 1 taller black highlight card on the right
- Middle row with 2 stacked small metric cards on the left, a wide revenue chart card in the center, and a to-do list section on the right
- Bottom row with a large recent-emails card and a smaller black event card on the bottom-right

Visual style:
- Use a very light cool gray / blue-gray page background
- Use cards that are slightly darker than the background, in a soft misty blue-gray tint
- Avoid pure white cards
- Avoid harsh borders
- Use very subtle or near-invisible shadows
- Use large rounded corners on all cards and containers
- Make the design feel calm, polished, and expensive
- Use black as the main high-contrast accent for the sidebar and key action cards
- Keep the interface minimal and airy, with lots of breathing room

Color direction:
- Background: pale cool gray-blue
- Cards: slightly darker pastel blue-gray
- High contrast surfaces: near-black
- Text: black for primary, muted gray for secondary
- Small status pills in green and soft red/pink

Box treatment:
- Large-radius rounded rectangles
- Soft monochrome tint layering
- Cards should feel embedded into the background through gentle tonal difference rather than strong shadows
- Minimal border visibility
- Flat-premium aesthetic, not glassmorphism, not neumorphism, not heavily shadowed

Typography:
- Bold friendly sans-serif headline
- Large bold KPI numbers
- Muted labels and metadata
- Clean modern SaaS dashboard feel

Overall mood:
- premium fintech dashboard
- calm executive interface
- soft pastel surfaces with black anchors
- highly polished, minimal, modern, and structured
