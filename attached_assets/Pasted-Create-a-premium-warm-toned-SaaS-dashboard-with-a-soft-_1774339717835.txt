Create a premium warm-toned SaaS dashboard with a soft ivory and cream color system, subtle orange/amber highlights, and a polished client-facing business workspace aesthetic.

Layout:
- One large rounded master container centered on a light neutral gray outer background
- Left vertical sidebar with brand, navigation, bottom utility links, and logout
- Main content area with a top greeting/header row
- Search bar and utility icons in the top-right
- Row of 4 equal KPI cards below the header
- Large analytics chart card on the middle-left
- Vertical profit-insights summary card on the middle-right
- Wide performance table card on the bottom

Visual style:
- Warm premium dashboard
- Use cream, ivory, pale beige, and blush-neutral surface tones instead of pure white
- Keep the page elegant, calm, soft, and editorial
- Use orange/amber/yellow as the main highlight/accent family
- Avoid harsh contrast, strong blue tones, or heavy dark panels
- Use very subtle borders and minimal shadows
- Everything should feel clean, spacious, and high-end

Color direction:
- outer background: very light neutral gray
- main container: warm ivory / off-white
- sidebar: pale blush-beige
- cards: cream / soft ivory / pale beige
- active nav: warm orange to yellow gradient
- icon circles: amber / orange gradient
- chart colors: soft orange area + yellow accent line
- positive indicators: green
- neutral text: soft charcoal and warm gray

Box treatment:
- large rounded rectangles
- cards separated more by tint and spacing than by shadow
- soft pill buttons and rounded search bar
- subtle borders, almost invisible
- flat-premium rather than heavily elevated

Typography:
- clean modern sans-serif
- bold greeting and KPI numbers
- medium-weight card titles
- muted supporting labels
- elegant SaaS dashboard hierarchy

Mood:
- premium client portal
- modern agency/productivity dashboard
- warm, soft, polished, and professional
- more welcoming than enterprise admin
