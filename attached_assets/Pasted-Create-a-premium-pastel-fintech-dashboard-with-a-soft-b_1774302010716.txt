Create a premium pastel fintech dashboard with a soft blue and mint color system.

Layout:
- One large rounded master container on a very light neutral background
- Thin top navigation bar with logo, nav links, search bar, utility icons, and avatar
- Main dashboard below arranged in a modular card grid
- Large hero finance card on the left for total balance, actions, and bank cards
- Smaller spending chart card in the upper middle
- Tall transactions list card on the upper right
- Small advice/CTA card below the spending card
- Large expenses chart card spanning the bottom-left
- Medium credit score summary card on the bottom-right

Visual style:
- Soft pastel consumer fintech aesthetic
- Use a pale powder-blue main container
- Use multiple tinted cards in the same pastel family instead of identical white cards
- Mix icy gray-blue cards, baby blue cards, and mint-sage cards
- Very large rounded corners on all cards and containers
- Extremely subtle or near-invisible shadows
- Minimal or invisible borders
- Flat-premium look, calm and elegant
- Do not use harsh contrast, deep shadows, or overly saturated colors

Color direction:
- outer background: soft warm off-white
- dashboard shell: pale powder blue
- main neutral cards: icy light gray-blue
- analytics cards: pastel blue
- supporting cards: mint / sage pastel
- small dark anchor elements in charcoal only where needed
- text: dark charcoal for primary, muted gray for secondary
- use blue and mint as soft accents

Box treatment:
- Rounded rectangles with gentle tonal separation
- Cards should feel embedded through tint variation, not strong elevation
- Each card group can have a slightly different pastel tint depending on purpose
- Keep the look airy, premium, and balanced

Typography:
- clean modern sans-serif
- large bold balance figure
- medium bold section titles
- muted supporting labels
- friendly fintech app feeling

Overall mood:
- premium digital banking dashboard
- soft pastel financial UI
- calm, trustworthy, modern, and polished
- consumer-friendly rather than admin-heavy
